PouchTaper — Support

Last updated: 10 July 2026

PouchTaper is a calm, private tool for tracking a gradual reduction ("taper") in your nicotine-pouch use. You set your own pace; the app keeps count and shows your progress. Everything stays on your device.

Wellness, not medical advice. PouchTaper is a self-help tracking tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and it makes no health or quit-success claims. If you have questions about nicotine use or your health, please speak with a qualified healthcare professional.

How the taper works

  • Set your plan. Tell PouchTaper roughly how many pouches you use per day now and how gradually you'd like to step down. The app builds a simple day-by-day target ramp — a target number of pouches that eases downward over time.
  • Log as you go. Each day, tap to log a pouch. The Today screen shows your count against that day's target with supportive, non-judgmental copy — being over target is framed as "tomorrow's a fresh start," never as failure.
  • Watch the line go down. The Progress screen charts your actual use against your target ramp, shows an estimate of money saved versus your starting pace, and marks simple milestones as you reach them.
  • Export any time. You can export your full pouch log as a CSV file from the About screen — free, no purchase required.

Your data & privacy

PouchTaper stores your plan and logs only on your device. It has no accounts, no servers, and no analytics or tracking. Nothing you enter is ever sent to us or to anyone else. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

Common questions

How do I change my plan?
Open the Plan screen, adjust your starting amount or pace, and generate an updated target ramp.
How do I back up or move my data?
Use the CSV export on the About screen to save a copy of your log wherever you like.
Does it work offline?
Yes. PouchTaper is fully on-device and needs no internet connection to work.
